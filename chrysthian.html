Who am I?

A guy who loves games to a point where to play them is not enough. So as a hobby, while some people write novels, others play music or do sports, I make games.

In Brazil, where I live, game development is not widely known as a real job. There are few offers on the area, almost none with reliable opportunities. Currently I work as a Web Application Programmer and, although it's not gaming related, it pays the bills.

After graduating on Systems Analysis I got a 2 year course on game development where a could learn from awesome teachers and professionals. Some of them had games published worldwide, reaching moderate success. During the studies I learned many things, applicable both inside and outside of a game development scenario. Quoting Einstein: "As our circle of knowledge expands, so it does the circumference of darkness surrounding it."

Here is a place where I'll try to share what I've learned and try not to forget them myself. Bear with me that most of what I write is based on my own learning experiences and I'm just passionately curious about lots of things.
